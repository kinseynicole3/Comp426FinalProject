What is Sparc?

Sparc is the Wikipedia and Reddit of Chapel Hill parking. Members come come online and add, delete, or edit any parking spaces, lots, or decks they know of in Chapel Hill. Its our mission that through this site UNC students can find safe, affordable, and convenient parking!


Why did we start Sparc?

Bryan Guin

"As an out of state student who drives to and from school every semester, having a car is essential. I also am a business major and consequently must drive to class quite often. My freshman year, I even had to pay a random person on Craigslist thousands of dollars to park my car for the year. That’s why I helped create Sparc, a website that democratizes parking knowledge and allows users to find non-traditional lots to park in.”

Kinsey Ness

"As someone who Uber’s to class when I don’t have anywhere to park, building this website was very important to me. I’m always looking for ways to avoid having to walk to class/campus, and I think a lot of college students feel the same way. UNC’s parking website is helpful to find places to park, but our website actually allows you to find parking that you wouldn’t otherwise know about. I think its super helpful, and has the potential to expand.”

Owen Riley

"Freshman year I spent a ton of money on transportation because I was not able to find parking. When I finally decided to just bring my car sophomore year I constantly was berated with tickets and warnings about getting my car towed for parking in incorrect spots that had improper or not up-to-date restrictions posted. To combat this, I helped create this site to help UNC students find accessible, cheap and maybe even free parking.”



Who is Sparc for?

Sparc is for whoever needs it! We made it for Chapel Hill students, but recognize that parking is crucial to the safety, convenince, and wallets of all Chapel Hill residents.

How can I contribute to Sparc?

You can contribute to Sparc by signing up and beggining to add spots to our database! You are also encouraged to leave any notes or comments on the spots we currently have, as we are always looking to have the most in-depth information and facts for all of our parking areas.
